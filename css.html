OUR OPTIONS

SHORT TRIP

FOR BEGINNERS

This kind of hiking is suitable for peole who are just getting started. That because it uses short routes and less peaks. These routes are also very close to the main cites. Like that those beginers would not have to travel long ways to attend. These outings usually start early morning or late afternoon when it's less hot. Depending on the trail the entire trip would have around 1 to 2 km.


MIDDLE TRIP

SUITABLE FOR EVERAGE HIKERS

This kind of hiking is suitable for peole who already went hiking a few times. That because it uses a little longer routes and some peaks. These routes are also close to the main cites. These locations make a lot easy for people to keep coming back for More not having to travel a long way to attend. These outings usually start early morning or early afternoon so that there is enough time for poeple to come back. Depending on the trail the entire trip would have around 2 to 4km.



LONG TRIP

ON THE MOVE HIKERS

This was section is for those who hike at least twice a month. The routes are longer than usual and there are often peaks to climb. Being so its is not always easy to find trail near the cities. Reason why most of this hiking programs are requested by our members. we usually dont have any specific time to depart or return. The trails are usually above 2km. We do not recomend any beginers or Elderly people to join.
