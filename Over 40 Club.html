WHY OVER 40 CLUB & HOW TO JOIN
JOINING THIS CLUB

Over 40 club tends to be our busiest clubs. I guess when age goes up we tend to care more about keeping fit. Here people tend to do less or shorter trips but real often. we also advise do hiking often in a shorter trips rather than one long trip done once in a while. Because of constant hiking programs we and the club members create many more other activities compared to the Youth Club.

To join simple send an email to faizalre@yahoo.com.br mentioning you name, age, location and things that you migth be interested in. Our group manager will find a group that suits you best.
